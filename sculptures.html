Featured Piece

Bezoar

2026 · Clay · Interactive 3D Model

Object inspired by Lorestan Bronze artifacts, bezoar ibex posess the largest horns of any fauna. They are native to the Zagros mountains of Iran, and their distinctive curved horns have been a recurring motif in my work. This piece was hand-sculpted from clay, it serves as an incense burner. Finally, it was 3D scanned and rendered for interactive viewing.
